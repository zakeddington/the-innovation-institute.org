William H. Arthur

Board Member

Bill's experience is largely in real estate development and consulting. He has also advised non-profit organizations in start-up, strategic planning, and financial management.

Bill is involved with the Institute because of his long-standing relationship with Harrold. He says, "Harrold has a great gift and passion to do this and I want to do what I can to give him an opportunity to express those gifts and that passion."

Bill's knowledge and experience were a great help to the Board in its formative period, and to Harrold through encouragement, sharing of wisdom and creative thinking.

Bill earned a BBA (magna cum laude) from the University of Texas at Arlington in Real Estate and an MBA from Southern Methodist University. He and his wife Julie live in Boerne, Texas. They have four children.
