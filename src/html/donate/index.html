Donations

You can make a difference in the lives of people living with paralysis.

Cash Donations

We are asking you to make a special tax-deductible donation today. By doing so, you will be making a difference in the lives of people living with disabilities.

  • $75

    Provides one day of wages for a disabled craftsman.

  • $250

    Will allow us to design and build a small assistive device for a needy individual.

  • $800

    Will cover the expenses to transport our carts to a camp where our disabled craftsmen will give rides and encouragement to children with disabilities.

  • $3900

    Pays for the materials to build another accessible cart.

  • $7400

    Would allow us to purchase an encloses trailer to transport our carts.

Other Donations

In addition to financial donations, you can donate:

  • Electric Wheelchairs
  • Hand tools and power tools
  • Riding lawnmowers and golf carts
  • Trucks and automobiles

You can also donate your time by:

  • Volunteering at special events
  • Assisting with fundraising and public relations
  • Organizing and participating in work site projects
